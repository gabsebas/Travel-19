What is Travel-19?

Travel-19 is a platform in which you can recive lastest updates on travel information and restrictions in the United states, state by state. Our tracker helps the general public be able to track the rates of the Coronavirus in different states. Our goal is to help make sure in case of any travel emegency, you're safe and prepared.
